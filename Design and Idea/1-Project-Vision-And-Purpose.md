# 1. Project Vision & Purpose

## What this project is about
SkillSync is a comprehensive, modern SaaS-style learning analytics platform designed to help users track, monitor, and analyze their learning journeys. It provides a cohesive ecosystem where individuals can log study sessions, set goals, manage skills, and visualize their progress over time. Built with React on the frontend and Spring Boot on the backend, SkillSync emphasizes a professional, Gamified, and data-driven approach to personal development.

## Why this project exists
In the modern world, continuous learning is essential, yet many learners struggle to stay consistent, measure their true progress, or recognize signs of burnout. SkillSync exists to bridge this gap by providing an intelligent system that not only tracks what you learn but *how* you learn. It replaces scattered notes and disjointed study timers with a centralized, insightful platform.

## What problem it solves
- **Lack of Visibility:** Learners often don't know how much time they've actually spent mastering a specific skill. SkillSync's dashboards and analytics provide clear visibility.
- **Burnout:** Overtraining without sufficient rest leads to burnout. SkillSync's burnout detection algorithms proactively warn users to maintain sustainable habits.
- **Disorganization:** Keeping track of various skills, categories, and goals can be overwhelming. SkillSync organizes everything into intuitive categories and interconnected roadmaps.
- **Isolation:** Learning alone can reduce motivation. SkillSync introduces leaderboards, shareable achievements, and study groups to foster a community vibe.

## Who the target users are
- **Self-Taught Developers & Professionals:** Individuals upskilling for career advancement who need structured tracking.
- **Students:** University or college students managing multiple subjects and exam preparations.
- **Lifelong Learners:** Hobbyists and enthusiasts dedicating time to learn new languages, instruments, or arts.
- **Educators/Mentors:** Professionals who want to monitor the learning velocity and progress of their mentees via groups and analytics.

## Real-world use case
Imagine a junior software engineer wanting to transition into a full-stack role. They can use SkillSync to:
1. Create a "Full-Stack Development" category.
2. Add specific skills like "React", "Spring Boot", and "PostgreSQL" with target mastery levels.
3. Set a Learning Goal: "Reach Level 3 in Spring Boot by Q3".
4. Log daily 1-hour learning sessions.
5. Use the Weekly Activity Heatmap to ensure consistent effort without triggering the Burnout Risk detector.
6. Share their generated achievement card on LinkedIn once the goal is reached.

## Business or technical purpose
**Business Purpose:** To serve as a high-value SaaS product offering premium analytics, group collaboration, and enterprise administration features for learning management.
**Technical Purpose:** To demonstrate a robust, scalable, and modern tech stack (React 19 + Vite + TailwindCSS 4.1 + Spring Boot 4 + PostgreSQL) complete with secure authentication (JWT), complex relational data modeling, and performance-optimized queries.

## Core goals of the project
1. Provide highly accurate and actionable learning analytics.
2. Ensure a premium, frictionless, and visually stunning user experience (Dark Mode, Gamification).
3. Foster sustained engagement through gamified elements (Streaks, Leaderboards, Badges).
4. Maintain a scalable and secure backend architecture capable of supporting extensive data aggregation.

## Key features and capabilities
- **Dashboard & Heatmaps:** GitHub-style contribution heatmaps for learning activity.
- **Skills & Categories Management:** Hierarchical tracking of competencies.
- **Session Tracking:** Granular time-tracking with timestamps and session notes.
- **Goal Setting:** Deadline-driven learning targets.
- **Burnout Detection:** Health-first monitoring of study intensity.
- **Gamification & Social:** Leaderboards, shareable progress cards (PNG/PDF), and peer ranking.
- **Collaboration:** Study groups, discussion forums, and collaborative event planning.
- **Admin Dashboard:** Comprehensive system monitoring, user management, and audit logs.

## Main user journey
1. **Onboarding:** User registers, customizes their profile, and sets their initial learning interests.
2. **Setup:** User creates their first few Categories (e.g., "Programming") and Skills ("Java").
3. **Action:** User logs daily Learning Sessions against those skills.
4. **Analysis:** User reviews the Analytics tab to see their velocity, strengths, and burnout risks.
5. **Achievement:** User completes a Goal, receives a notification, ranks up on the Leaderboard, and exports their Achievement Card to share online.
